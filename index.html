The History of the Imperium Working Group New Zealand (HIWG NZ), was the NZ chapter supporting development of materials for the Traveller roleplaying game. This microsite was derived from the original site (from the 1990s) maintained by Martin Rait of FSpace Publications for it. This covered the late era of work on Yiklerdanzh sector (HIWG OZ in which New Zealand was participating) and the later Meshan Sector landgrant to Martin Rait for HIWG NZ. The site went offline in the early 21st century after the group ceased to exist.

The content for the site is no longer available in web format here. We had some permissions withdrawn by one participant. The rest of the material either appears in The Meshan Saga issues from the era, or has been released in a series of free Meshan sector fan publications or repurposed into new Cepheus and non-Traveller commercial products. The links to all known materials released by the remaining HIWGNZ team at FSpace Publications is provided on this site. More will be released in the future.

FSpace Publications once held a licence from Marc Miller to publish several CT LBBs for the Meshan Sector. That project collapsed - however some of the material has emerged in new contexts, with them linked from this site.

If you'd like to contact FSpace Publications go to their contact form.
